What I Do

Research Areas

Integrating AI, remote sensing, and physics-based modeling to tackle the most pressing global water challenges.

Hydrological Modeling

Large-scale hydrological simulations integrating physics-based models (VIC, MOSART, E3SM) with satellite observations to predict water availability and understand the global water cycle under climate change.

VIC Model MOSART E3SM Global Water Cycle

AI / ML for Water Systems

Developing deep reinforcement learning and neural network frameworks for intelligent reservoir operations, streamflow forecasting, and adaptive management of river systems under uncertainty.

Deep Learning Reinforcement Learning LSTM Data Assimilation

Climate Resilience & Adaptation

Assessing climate change impacts on water infrastructure and developing adaptive strategies for drought and flood risk mitigation across urban and rural water systems at global scale.

Drought Management Flood Risk Urban Water Climate Adaptation

Transboundary Water Management

AI-driven optimization for resolving international water-sharing conflicts and managing multi-objective reservoir operations in shared river basins across Africa, Asia, and the Middle East.

River Basins Multi-objective Opt. Remote Sensing Water Policy
